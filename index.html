About GG Enterprises

GG Enterprises was started in 2008 with an aim to provide effective and innovative waterproofing solutions. We provide innovative and trusted building waterproofing solutions with innovative products from Insuwrap, Firestone Building Products, Saveto and Colmef.

Our Services

Our service range includes waterproofing solutions like PVC Membranes, TPO and EPDM Membranes, Construction Chemicals, liquid waterproofing and Building and Finishing products.

Roofing

Insuwrap PVC WM/WMR-UV is a calendered extruded high polymer waterproofing membrane, not compatible with bitumen, manufactured following highest International standards offering high physical properties and long term durability.

Portable Water tanks

Insuwrap PVC water tank internal liner LNT/LNTR is a calendered extruded high polymer waterproofing membrane reinforced with polyester mesh, not compatible with bitumen, and is manufactured using the best raw materials produced by SABIC.

Pool Liners

Insuwrap PVC 1500 LR UV is a calendered extruded high polymer waterproofing membrane reinforced with high tenacity polyester mesh and ultraviolet resistant, not compatible with bitumen, manufactured following highest International standards offering high physical properties and long term durability.

Underground Structures

Insuwrap PVC underground waterproofing membrane 1500 TNL is a calendered extruded high polymer waterproofing membrane reinforced with polyester mesh, not compatible with bitumen, and is manufactured using the best raw materials produced by SABIC.
